These recipes sound delicious.Here is another caramel flavoured icecream: a crema catalana icecream that has dark praline in it. 500ml simple cream1 cinammon stickgrated lemon peelbring these to a simmer and allow to cool and infuse. Add a couple of drops or orange blossom water.beat 3 egg yolks with 65g sugar until pale and cook over low heat until coats a spoon.caramelize 100g sugar until dark brown (or to taste). Allow to cool and smash into a praline.Add half of the praline (or less if you wish a less intense caramel taste) to the custard and freeze in an icecream maker. Mix in the remaining praline at the end.Not the same texture, but it does remind me or real crema catalana.happy thanksgiving, Molly
