]]> By: Tony http://orangette.net/2006/05/in-praise-of-poaching/#comment-4845 Mon, 29 May 2006 08:11:00 +0000 https://elitemporaryblog.wordpress.com/2006/05/09/in-praise-of-poaching#comment-4845 Great Blog. Another way to cook fish similar to poaching is the “plate method”. Simply place a plate with the fish fillet, some flavourings (lemon juice, olive oil, pepper, ginger, coriander, whatever takes you fancy) over a pot of water on the boil. Cover with another plate. When the top plate is warm hot to touch, the fish is done. Clean flavours, no mess, no fried fish smell. What could be easier?! We don’t have halibut in Australia but ocean trout and salmon work very well.
