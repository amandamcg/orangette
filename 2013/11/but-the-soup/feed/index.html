I’d like to roast my butternut squash on the Kamado, then use it to make the soup. to prepare for the grill, I peel off the sticker and toss it over the fire. I figure that the extra and unique flavor of the smoked squash will more than offset any loss of flavor by not cooking the raw squash within the soup. Another revelation is that I no longer have to deal with peeking the wretched shell.
