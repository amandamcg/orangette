The ingredients are largely the same, but we add an important one, time. Pour your gin of choice into a tall glass. Add the juice of half a lime, and the lime rind. Muddle, to release the rind’s oils, for :30 seconds. Then wait for a minimum of two minutes for everything to magically soften. (Ever eaten ceviche?)Add a dash of bitters. Add fresh ice cubes, then slowly pour very fresh tonic from a fresh, small bottle (or fresh can; Whole Foods makes decent tonic) gently down the side of the glass. Add a fresh spritz of lime, and enjoy blissful repose, witty banter, and the light from the slanting sun. -DRC
