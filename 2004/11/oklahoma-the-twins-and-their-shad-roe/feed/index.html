I have only read Orangette, but I look forward to reading more as time permits.The way I eventually prepared my shad roe was to soak it and some tilapia fillets in heavy cream while I sautéed some shitake mushrooms, asparagus shallots, garlic, chopped parsley, and chopped chives in butter and white wine. I seasoned with salt and black pepper. After they softened a bit I placed the tilapia fillets in the same pan. A minute or so later I added the shad roe and enough of the heavy cream to make a nice sauce.
