]]> By: Mickey https://orangette.net/2016/03/9471/#comment-51927 Thu, 31 Mar 2016 13:48:27 +0000 http://orangette.net/?p=9471#comment-51927 I live a stone’s throw from Dino’s, and for anyone who’s coming out of town: parking in this neighborhood is very tough. Here’s some great news: there is now a gorgeous light rail train stop 4 blocks away. It opened in March 2016, and is making Capitol Hill so much more accessible. Search Seattle light rail link schedules to find out if this is a viable transportation mode for you. If you’re coming from the north, you can leave your car in the university district, walk through the beautiful university campus and take light rail to the Capitol Hill station. If you’re coming from the south, light rail stretches all the way to Sea-Tac airport. Depending on where you exit, walk down the hill via John Street or Denny Way for 5 minutes. Both take you directly to the Olive Way intersection – and there’s Dino’s. Enjoy!
