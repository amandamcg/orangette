Anyhoo just wanted to pass on a rhubarb tip my granny taught me: after you've trimmed, washed and sliced the stalks, pour boiling water onto it and leave for a minute or so. Then drain and proceed with whatever you're going to use the 'barb for. The boiling water treatment seems to reduce the tartness and you can get away with using much less sugar.

I like to bake rhubarb and blueberries together – it's a good combination.
