I don’t stop harvesting mine until late October into November. DO NOT CUT RHUBARB STALKS. Pull them. Yep – just pull them. If you do that, you will have rhubarb into the Fall. When you cut the stalk, new stalks cannot grow. If you pull the stalk out(gentle tugging, it will let you know if it’s ready to be picked)a new stalk will form in that spot. Try it and you’ll have ‘barb all summer long.
