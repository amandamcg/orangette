]]> By: Anonymous http://orangette.net/2015/02/et-voila/#comment-43716 Mon, 23 Feb 2015 15:57:30 +0000 https://elitemporaryblog.wordpress.com/2015/02/14/et-voila#comment-43716 Love this. Also, as veteran hot cocoa maker, I would add: I really do recommend the water method that folks have referred to (and that appears on the container of Hershey cocoa – we can all agree that their cocoa is not the best, but they have nailed this recipe). Water, as opposed to milk, really brings out the chocolate flavor. Hence, Like Water for Chocolate. Mix cocoa plus sugar, add just enough water to dissolve it, and heat until simmering and cocoa flavor explodes (like garlic in oil, kind of). Then add milk and proceed. This can all be done handily in the microwave. I think a splash of vanilla at the end is not unwarranted. Also, I think some salt kicks it all up–a generous pinch improves the flavor. Just some thoughts–either way, June will have delicious cocoa and continue to be utterly adorable!
