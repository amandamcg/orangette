I swiveled around in my chair to stare gravely at my boyfriend Kevin (visiting from Colorado to help me move my surprisingly car-sized life to Orcas Island – and to be my boyfriend) and asked if we could have pizza at Delancy.

I had never been, having grown up in California, and it felt somehow so close (because yea) and so right (because I don’t know?)

It was perfect. It was. Kevin has pizza once a week (sometimes twice – possibly even three times and I just don’t know) and mumbled with cheese dangling from his lips that “this is the best pizza that I have ever had.” I tried to swallow my sip of negroni without choking on the smile I was pouring into the glass.

I’m not really sure what it was I wanted to – or what it is that I could say – the feeling is I think too big with too many names. It tastes an awful lot like love, even if ” thank you for existing.” is all that comes out. So – thank you for existing, Molly.
