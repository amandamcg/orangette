I guess I’m solidly a West Coast girl that way…Anonymous, I’ve heard a number of different opinions on the issue of whether to buy Dungeness crab cooked or live, and there are pros and cons to both. When live crabs are kept in tanks in stores, they are not fed, so they often lose weight and succulence, a definite con. I generally choose to buy cooked ones from a source that I trust, a source that deals directly with fishermen (who often cook the crabs immediately after catching, on their boats), chooses the crabs carefully, and sells them as fresh as can be.
