The version I make (from a BBC cooking show but I forget which) calls for mustard and cumin seeds to be fried in hot oil til they sputter. The mix is then poured on the julienned carrots and kohlrabi (half of which would have already been devoured as a snack during the prep). Add lemon juice. Toss. Eat.
