]]> By: jessica. https://orangette.net/2011/08/it-still-surprises-me/#comment-29362 Sat, 17 Sep 2011 01:53:52 +0000 https://elitemporaryblog.wordpress.com/2011/08/14/it-still-surprises-me#comment-29362 I've made these a few times now, with different fruit each time (PEACHES, y'all, do it), and I've found this little tweak to the method really helps to cure that crushing-the-berries ailment:

Toss your fruit into the dry mixture to blend it well, and then pour the vanilla'ed HnH over top (drizzle it around a bit). I let it sit for about two minutes, then come back and give it a quick 1-2-3 stir, and it's perfect!
