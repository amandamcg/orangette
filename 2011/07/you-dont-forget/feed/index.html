]]> By: Annie https://orangette.net/2011/07/you-dont-forget/#comment-30108 Mon, 07 May 2012 16:22:08 +0000 https://elitemporaryblog.wordpress.com/2011/07/18/you-dont-forget#comment-30108 Molly, I don't see why you could not can this recipe. You can BWB can fruit packed in light syrup. You can even pack berries in water, apparently. The one thing I would probably be sure is that the syrup is still juicy enough to cover everything, which it looks like it is. Here's another link to a site that is very food-safety oriented, and which has a recipe for canning strawberries at a rate of 1/2 cup sugar to 1 quart (4 cups) fruit: http://pickyourown.org/strawberries_canning.php. The only thing the conserve does is cook down the syrup to make it denser, which shouldn't change the acidity.

Hope this helps. I plan to try this recipe soon as strawberries are in season again!
