Let me explain.
Back in 2019 I dmed you on instagram as I was trying to get an early copy of the fixed stars . My mom was slowly dying of cancer back then and being a very loyal reader for years , she were convinced she would not be around to read your book originally planned to be released in may 2020 if i’m not mistaken . So I contacted you and was very surprised to get a message from you and even more, a signed copy that would travel all the way from the US to my doorstep here in france . It’s been quite some time . My mom passed away shortly after receiving her copy but she got a chance to read it from cover to cover and even discuss it with me . Earlier this week I was going through some of her belongings and I found the book . I opened it and found a note . I recognised her handwriting . It roughly translates to ” I wish you to know that this book was one of the last beautigul things I have encountered by the end of my life “.
I had to let you know . I would want the whole world to know that your act of kindness to a complete stranger will always be associated in my memory to those last few weeks with her .
Thank you for your generosity . You are a beautiful person , I hope you know it by now
